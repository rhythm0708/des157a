Sandbox Games!

Sandbox games offer a lot of freedom with regards to when you play, how you play, and who you play with. Games such as Minecraft and Roblox contain free-build modes where you can build and create worlds to your heart's content. The Sims series famously offers the ability for players to control and develop the lives and relationships of their virtual characters. Games such as No Man's Sky and Grand Theft Auto can also be considered sandbox as they are open-ended and offer infinite ways to play. If you enjoy unleashing creativity and expressing your mind, this genre might be for you!
