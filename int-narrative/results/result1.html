Alternate Reality Games!

Alternate Reality Games (also known as ARGs) are a genre of games that uses the real-world as a platform to host a game in real-time. Popular formats include interactive theater, murder mystery, and scavenger hunt. There have also been large-scale ARGs such as Why So Serious? and Matpat's 7-Gates that take place over several days, locations, and platforms.
