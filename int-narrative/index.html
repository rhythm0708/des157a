The Gamer Personality Quiz

By Lucas Wang (@rhythm07)

Play is an intrinsic part of human nature. Primates, mammals, fish, and birds all play, and benefit from the cognitive, social, and physical advantages it provides.

Recently I have noticed that many of our lives seem to lack substantial play, especially past childhood. Whether it is a puzzle game to start the morning or a group game for family gatherings, play can benefit us all and create a healthy mind. So this quiz tries to answer: what type of game is right for you? This quiz aims to match visitors with a type of game that is satisfying and enjoyable based on their personality. Note: This quiz is not designed based on any science or empirical evidence; it is purely subjective and based on my observations alone.
