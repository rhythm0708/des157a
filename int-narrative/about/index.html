The Gamer Personality Quiz

About The Gamer Personality Quiz

What is this?

This is a short quiz designed to find a game that matches your personality type. All you have to do is answer a series of eight questions and my website will do the rest. Note: this is not a scientific or evidence-based quiz. It is based purely on my subjective opinion and should only be used to gain a lighthearted opinion.

Who are you?

Hey, my name is Lucas. Online, you might see me as rhythm or rhythm07. I'm a current student studying Design, Games, and Interactive Media at the University of California, Davis. I'm passionate about puzzle games, social games, and analog games. I like to create things that make people laugh, think, and say wow, why didn't I think of that? I'm the co-founder of Studio Blocket and the principal game designer for games on my itch page.

Why did you make this?

This is my final project for DES 117 - Interactive Media. We were tasked with designing an interactive webpage that users could click through in a meaningful progression. For this project, I wanted to create something engaging that involved more than just reading. A simple website that I would enjoy using. A wesite that provided both meaning and entertainment to its users. Although the idea itself is a little silly, I thought that it'd be a nice challenge to develop and one that would test my skills.
