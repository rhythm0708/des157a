Case 1: Who destroyed Hannah's project?

Story

On November 1st 2024, Professor Sandberg and his students entered the lab to find it in a state of disarray. Candy was scattered across the floor, random objects were everywhere, and Hannah's project lay on its rack, smashed beyond recognition.

Hannah was furious. She pointed fingers at everyone around her. "Who did this?" she cried, "Which one of you ruined my project?"

Professor Sandberg tried to calm her down. "Calm down Hannah," he consoled, "Yelling won't help us find the culprit. What we need to do is search the room for clues. There are only a few suspects. After all, not many people have access to this room, and the door is locked at night.

Can you figure out who ruined Hannah's project?

The case is solved

Upon being confronted by the class, Hannah burst into tears.

"I...I wanted an extension on the project, but the professor wouldn't give it to me," she confessed. "Last night I wasn't thinking clearly and I...made a bad choice."

She took a shaky breath and continued, "I was the last one in the lab last night, and I realized I could turn the situation to my advantage. I scattered the candy to make it look like someone else had done it in a fit of rage."

Professor Sandberg looked around at the messy room, shaking his head. "As your punishment," he declared, "You will spend the next two weeks cleaning up after class."
