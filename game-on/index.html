Rules

Tired of traditional rock paper scissors?

Each player starts off with 2 Rocks, 2 Papers, and 2 Scissors in their hand. They play rock, paper, scissors as normal. But here’s the twist: if you lose a round with a move, then that move is gone for good! The last player with moves left wins the game.

If this sounds like a bad idea, that’s because it is. It was inspired by this clip on Alpharad’s channel.

But in general:

  1. Each round, choose one move to play.
  2. If a move ever loses, it is gone for the rest of the game.
  3. You lose the game when you run out of moves.
